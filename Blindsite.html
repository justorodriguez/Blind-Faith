About Us

Our charity consists of over 40,000 members worldwide. Our global community is an active part in helping us on our mission to provide service to as many as possible. We create and manage numerous fundraisers and events all over to fund projects that will omprove the lives of those in need.

What We Do

We here at Blind Foundation are dedicated to help those who are affected with blindness and other visual disabilities. We gather and raise funds that go directly to treatment, research, and equipment to aid those who are visually impaired.
